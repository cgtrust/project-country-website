Melbourne

Melbourne is known for its coffee, and the biggest tram network system in the world. There are over 500 trams with 250kms of track. The central part of the tram network is free, between Queen Victoria Market and Flinders Street. Flinders Street Station is a popular meeting place for locals. It is one of the busiest transport stations in the southern hemisphere, with around 110,000 people per day passing through the hub. Make sure you also stop by Federation Square just a few blocks away! Every year, Melbourne is host to the Australian Open major tennis tournament. Known as a Grand Slam, this takes place at Melbourne Park. The major arena inside, Rod Laver Arena is named after a local tennis legend.
